WELCOME TO NECTO

Necto is a legendary nightclub located in the heart of Ann Arbor, Michigan. With its state-of-the-art sound system, pulsating lights, and cutting-edge atmosphere, Necto is the ultimate destination for nightlife in Michigan. Our resident DJs spin the hottest tracks, ensuring that the dance floor is always packed. We offer an array of signature cocktails and premium drinks at our fully-stocked bar, served by our friendly and professional staff. Whether you're looking for a wild night out with friends or a more intimate experience in one of our VIP lounges, Necto has something for everyone. As one of the most popular clubs in Michigan, Necto draws in visitors from all over the state and beyond. So if you're looking for an unforgettable night of dancing, music, and fun, come and experience the magic of Necto.

ABOUT THE CLUB

Established in 2001, the Necto is Ann Arbor's premier destination for nightlife entertainment. Located in the heart of Ann Arbor's entertainment district, Necto is the nightlife epicenter for the University of Michigan, the city of Ann Arbor, and the community at large.

The Necto offers multiple weekly and special events to maximize your nightlife experience including the internationally known goth night Factory Monday, the unique concert and event-driven Mix Thursdays, the largest and longest established gay night in Michigan, Pride Fridays, and the hottest club night in Michigan, Frequency Saturdays.

As an award-winning, full-service venue, the club is proud to offer clientele a full range of entertainment options and amenities, including VIP, bottle service, and party packages to ensure your night is memorable and the ability to make your own night through our private event rental.
